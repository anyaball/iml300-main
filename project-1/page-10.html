Anxiety in it's simplest forms

Has no mercy, on especially the youngest of minds. Is it really ever okay?

The smallest of things build an ache in your stomach, a pain in your head, and tension in your muscles.

Indecisiveness grips your throat with a strong hand.

Tears seek their way out with each passing moment.

And eventually pushes your body to find home.

To find your bedroom.

To find your bed.

To fall into the comforting and warm pit your blankets form around you.

And pull tightly around you in a warm embrace.

Almost suffocating you as the feeling creeps up again.

Holding you prisoner to your own comfort space.

Trapped in your own mind as the ache builds in your stomach.

The pain rushes your head.

The tension builds in your muscles.

And you are stuck once again to the mercy of your own mind.

Will it ever be okay?
